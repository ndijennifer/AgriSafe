# AgriSafe
AgriSafe is an Android app built for rural farmers in Cameroon to log daily farming activities, monitor crop health through photo capture, and stay protected from marketplace scams. The app uses four built-in phone sensors , the Camera to photograph crops and detect diseases, GPS to map and tag farm locations, the Accelerometer to log activities with a simple phone shake, and the Microphone to record voice notes hands-free. It works fully offline so farmers in areas with poor internet can still use every feature, and includes automated unit testing to ensure the app is reliable.
